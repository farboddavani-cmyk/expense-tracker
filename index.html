How to link to Excel
  1. Download the expense_tracker.xlsx file to your computer
  2. Log expenses here in the app all month
  3. Tap Download CSV when ready to update Excel
  4. Open Excel → Expense Log sheet → click cell A5
  5. Data → From Text/CSV → select your downloaded CSV
  6. The Dashboard & Tax Summary sheets update automatically
  7. At tax time, just share or print the Tax Summary sheet
